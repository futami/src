 521  *

 522  * Individual #SCH_SCREEN objects are unique and correspond to .sch files.

 523  *

 524  * NOTE: It may be desirable to fold the functionality of SCH_SCREENS into

 525  * the new SCHEMATIC class at some point, since SCHEMATIC can also be thought

 526  * of as owning the collection of all the SCH_SCREEN objects.
